Day Eleven

Charles Dickens' is a well-deserved Christmas classic. MORE HERE
  • Knit yourself a Scrooge/Krachit nightcap.
  • Once you've got your nightcap knitted, it's time to get into the story! If you've got a group of people at home, I recommend making it a multi-night event; turn the lights out, light a candle, and take turns reading aloud.
  • If you'd rather watch or listen than read: Patrick Stewart's one man show version is legendary. While I haven't been able to find a video of it, an audio recording is still available on Amazon. The 1999 TV movie featuring Stewart is pretty good as well.
  • If you're less of a traditionalist, you could also go for A Muppet Christmas Carol, in which case you may enjoy this accompanying drinking game.

Day Sixteen

For some people, the Christmas season is also the season for charitable giving. However, not all charities are created equal; see this Washington Post article about the fundraising song "Do They Know It's Christmas?".

So how to make sure your donation is doing the most good? Try Charity Navigator, which scores charities according to their transparency of records and financial practices; or try GiveWell.org, which suggests charities that are particularly worthy of a donation.

Day Five

Are you a fan of Christmas? And are you just crazy about tubas? If so, then you probably want to find the nearest Tubachristmas event.

More of a trombone person? Then you'll be happy to know that in addition to Tubachristmas, there's also Trombone Christmas, the "All Western SLIDE Trombone Christmas Concert". Their annual concert is a little over a week from today on Saturday December 12th in Annaheim, CA.
